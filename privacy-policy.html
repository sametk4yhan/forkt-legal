Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Forkt ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Forkt - Would You Rather mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Forkt:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID, name (if you choose to share it), and email address.
  • Profile Information: Username, country, and city that you provide during onboarding.
  • Game Data: Your answers to questions, answer times, scores, streaks, and personality tags.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and language preference.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app experience
  • To display leaderboards (world, country, and city rankings)
  • To calculate your personality tags and statistics
  • To show relevant advertisements (via Google AdMob)
  • To process in-app purchases (via RevenueCat / Apple)

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. If you grant tracking permission, personalized ads may be shown. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. You can also purchase "Remove Ads" to eliminate all advertisements.

4. In-App Purchases

Forkt offers a one-time lifetime purchase to remove ads. Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store and managed by RevenueCat. We do not store your payment information.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (cloud database hosted on AWS). We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising purposes
  • RevenueCat: For purchase management
  • Apple: For authentication and purchases
  • Supabase: For data storage

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account and all associated data from within the app (Settings > Delete Account)
  • Opt out of ad tracking via the iOS tracking permission dialog
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us

8. Children's Privacy

Forkt is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
